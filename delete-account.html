Account & Data Deletion — Grumble Dome

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Grumble Dome account and the data associated with it. Grumble Dome is published by the Grumble Dome developer. If you have any trouble, contact us at GrumbleDomeSupport@gmail.com.

How to delete your account from inside the app

  1. Open Grumble Dome on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings button on the main menu.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Complete the confirmation steps (confirming your Grumbler name, confirming your chip total, a press and hold, and your device screen lock). These steps are there to make sure the deletion is really you.

Once confirmed, your account and its data are permanently deleted right away. This cannot be undone.

How to request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email GrumbleDomeSupport@gmail.com from the address associated with your account and ask us to delete your account. We will verify and remove your data.

What data is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of the data we hold for it, including:

  • Your account and sign in record
  • Your player name and profile
  • Your game progress, rank, decks, owned items, statistics, and settings
  • Your friends, friend requests, and online presence

What is kept, and for how long

We do not keep a copy of your game data after deletion, and there is no additional retention period on our side: the data is removed at the time you confirm the deletion.

Records of any purchases you made are held by Google Play under Google's own policies and are outside our control; we never store your payment card details. Server logs that do not identify you may persist briefly for security and reliability before being overwritten.

Contact
