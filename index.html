Privacy Policy for Grumble Dome

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Grumble Dome mobile game ("the App", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We only collect what is needed to save your progress, let you play online, and keep matches fair. Specifically:

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and a Google account identifier. We use these to identify your account and sync your progress.
  • Game and profile data. Your player name, in‑game progress, rank, decks, owned items, settings, statistics, and friends list. This is stored so your progress is saved and available across sessions.
  • Online play data. When you play online, your player name and in‑match moves are relayed in real time to your opponent so the match can run. Your online presence (whether you are online) and match results may be stored to support friends, matchmaking, and fair‑play verification.
  • Device and security information. A device/session identifier is used to keep your account signed in on one device at a time and to detect tampering or cheating. We do not collect your contacts, photos, location, or browsing history.
  • Purchase information. If you buy in‑app chip bundles, the purchase is processed by Google Play Billing. We receive confirmation that a purchase completed so we can grant your chips. We do not receive or store your credit card or payment details — Google handles all payment information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and save your game progress.
  • To enable online matchmaking, friends, and real‑time multiplayer matches.
  • To protect the game from cheating and abuse.
  • To process in‑app purchases and grant purchased items.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use it for advertising. The App does not contain third‑party ads or analytics/tracking SDKs.

Third‑Party Services

We rely on the following service providers, which process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) — sign‑in and cloud storage of your account and progress. See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Supabase (Realtime) — relays online matchmaking and in‑match moves between players. See Supabase's Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy.
  • Google Play Billing — processes in‑app purchases. See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your account and game data for as long as your account exists. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App: open Settings → Delete Account. This erases your stored progress, profile, friends, presence, and related records. You may also request deletion by emailing us at the address below, and we will remove your data.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use industry‑standard measures provided by our service providers, including encrypted connections and access controls, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
